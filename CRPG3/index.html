Visão de Negócio

Tronox Pigmentos do Brasil S.A. engages in the production and sale of titanium dioxide (TiO2) pigments worldwide. Its TiO2 pigments are used in paints, plastics, papers and rubbers, household equipment, toys, electronics, packaging, and others. It also produces ilmenite, zirconite, rutile, and kyanite ores. The company was founded in 1966 and is headquartered in Camaçari, Brazil. Tronox Pigmentos do Brasil S.A. operates as a subsidiary of Millennium Inorganic Chemicals Holdings Brasil LTDA.
